About Us

Welcome to Pawsitive Vibes, your trusted neighborhood pet care shop in the heart of CR Park, Delhi. We understand the unique bond you share with your pets and are committed to providing them with the highest standard of care.

Our philosophy is simple: to create a nurturing and stimulating environment where pets feel safe, loved, and happy. Our team comprises experienced and passionate pet care professionals dedicated to meeting the individual needs of every animal in our care.

We cater to the health-conscious families of Delhi, offering services and products that promote the overall well-being of your furry, feathered, and scaled companions. From premium nutrition to engaging daycare and gentle grooming, we're here to support you in providing the best possible life for your beloved pets.
